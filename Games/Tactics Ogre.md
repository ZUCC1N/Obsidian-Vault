# Game progression
in the valerian isles, the inhabitants have long thought for their land and defence, the region being a key naval commerce point. the conflict was brought to an end by a man, Dorgalua Oberyth, known as the Dynast-King, uniting the region and races under a single banner. but discontent persisted. upon his death, Abuna Brantyn Morne declared independence, forming Bakram with Heim as the capital, backed by the Holy Lodissian Empire to the north. but his conquest was hampered by Lanselot Tartaros, leader of the Dark Knights of Lodis, warning Abuna of his reckless expansion. due to this, the south remained unclaimed, the two clans Galgastani and Walister battling for dominion over the land. 7 in 10 were Galgastani, causing Hierophant Leundar Balbatos to order the genocide of Walister in order to form the kingdom of Galgastan in the south. Duke Juda Ronwey formed the Walister Resistance, battling against the extermination of his people, but was outmatched being brought to the brink of destruction in only half a year.  Balbatos declared his genocide complete, forming Galgastan and ending the war officially, but oppression of the people of Walister still persisted. the resistance remained but with the Duke captured, Morale dwindled. the Walister were pushed to a small province by Balbatos and the Galgastani, leaving the survivors to live their life as best they could under Galgastan rule. with this an uneasy peace was formed, equilibrium met with Lodis overseeing the region from the north, Bakram in the north and Galgastan having quelled the Walister rebellion.
but none could have believed this peace to remain for long.
## Chapter 1: There is Blood on my Hands, How Long Till it Lies on my Heart?
### Golyat
you see the peaceful town of Golyat settling down for the night. an Army looms over the town, entering the town and killing the inhabitants. Vyce rases to Denam and Catiua's Vyce home, informing them of Lanselot's return to Walister, rallying the two to attempt to kill him to have Bakram falter for a moment, despite Catiua believing this to be foolish, Denam taking a neutral approach. Catiua suggests it ill to incite another war so early after the previous ended, Vyce responding that the way they live now is so free of suffering, saying Galgastan sees them as pests to be killed. the band arrive, Vyce attacking them before realising them to not be Lanselot they believe him to be. this Lanselot claims to be from New Xenobia from the east of Valeria, and has 2 eyes unlike Lanselot Tartaros. this band is actually a group of exiled knights from New Xenobia, lead by Lanselot Hamilton, outlaws looking for work.

Catiua forgives their transgressions, the trio introducing themselves, Catiua saying she is a Sibyl. she invites them to their hideout, explaining their lack of infantry that Bakram also suffers from, only saved by Support from Lodis and their Dark Knight Order Loslorien which reports directly to High Priest Sardian, leader of Lodis. the band enquire to the resistance's movements, Denam informing them of Duke Ronwey held in Almorica, a fortress of Galgastan. they see their interests align, setting out to the castle, but after Catiua expresses her discontent with the men charging into battle foolhardy, they have seen enough death as it is. they dismiss her complaints, preparing for battle as Catiua asks why Denam cannot see the way she is thinking, she doesn't want to lose her brother, like they lost their father in Golyat. Catiua exclaims that Vyce is no noble warrior, dying on the backs of others before dying himself (which is probably foreshadowing)

### Almorica Castle
the warriors of New Xenobia assist you in entering the castle and rescuing the duke from shackles, dispatching the trap at the castle gate and the general within the keep Ronwey thanks the trio for his rescue, thanking the band for their assistance, with the concern that Warren matches the description of a diviner who assisted the King Tristan of New Xenobia 2 years ago, claiming the throne with him, this concept that such important men so easily cast out not sitting well with him. Catiua vouches for them, saying they risked their lives for them so that is all the evidence of their loyalty he needs. he accept this, giving them jobs in training soldiers for the resistance in Almorica. the Duke speaks to Denam of his father, Abuna Prancet, killed in the attack on Golyat. Catiua asks the duke to get revenge for their father, but he responds that Galgastan is the current threat, Bakram and Lodis can come later. the Duke offers the trio Knighthood at the table of Almorica, using their recent exploits as a driving force for the Walister masses. he gives them the task to travel to Krysaro, to track down the captain of the Duke's knights, Sir Leonar, who was chasing the former resident of the castle placed by Galgastan, Nybeth. they are to travel to Krysaro to aid him. he also gives them goth and men in order to allow smooth travel to Krysaro.

in the yard Lanselot thanks Denam for his assistance in employment. they wish the band good travels as Dame Ravness stops the trio's departure, jeering at Vyce's boastfulness of reclaiming the castle as it was only possible since Ravness had drawn the garrison defending it away. she offers words of wisdom as she leaves, Catiua commenting that she is prideful and hopes to not be around when she has her down fall (and then Denam and Vyce look at her like bitch what are you saying you got no chill).

### Krysaro
you arrive to Krysaro, expecting to find Nybeth but find Abuna Donnalto fighting a legion of undead, raised by Moldova, apprentice of Nybeth. they defeat her and meet Sir Leonar, thanking them for their assistance in fending off the undead. you are told Nybeth is held out in a fortress further beyond the town.
