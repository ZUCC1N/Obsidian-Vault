## Background
Nier Automata is heavily influenced by many authors and works, mainly in the realm of Philosophy such as [[Friedrich Nietzsche]], [[Immanuel Kant]],  [[Jean-Paul Sartre]], [[Blaise Pascal]],  [[Simone de Beauvoir]], [[Søren Kierkegaard]]. it mainly works to critique and examine the existentialist state of the world and what it means to be human in the framework of a world where humans are long dead, and what human like entities that remain struggle to obtain humanity in their own ways.
in the end it comes to say the only shared experience of life is pain, and the companionship in battling that pain for the greater good.
