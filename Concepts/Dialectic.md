dialectics originally referred to the conversation between two people holding opposing views, which relied on objective facts opposed to subjective ones. [[Georg Wilhelm Friedrich Hegel|Hegel]] retooled its definition of a dialogue of ideas opposed to a literal dialogue, being forces pushing and pulling on each other, creating contradictions in each other but also resolving them, creating change. this idea eventually went on to define Hegelian [[Dialectical Idealism]] and [[Karl Marx|Marx]] and [[Friedrich Engels|Engels]]'s [[Dialectical Materialism]].
