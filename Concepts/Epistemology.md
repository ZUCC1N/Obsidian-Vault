the study of the "theory of knowledge" in [[Philosophy]]. it focuses on the nature of knowledge how its obtained. it investigates knowledge compared to belief or opinion, and seeks to ascertain conditions where a claim is justified, by examining and challenging established knowledge. it along side [[Metaphysics]] are akin to the chemistry and physics of philosophy, in that they underpin a great amount of the field of philosophy relies on understanding these 2 fields at a base.
