in essence, Dialectical idealism relies first on an [[Idealism|Idealist]] pretense. from this, in a [[Dialectic|dialectical]] sense it can be reasoned that ""good"" ideas create good change, and ""bad"" ideas bad change. it is then the job of a thinker or philosopher to ponder ideas to create the ""best"" idea and thus invoke good change. but also, this implies all good history and progression was born from this, and all bad history born from misinterpreting those ideas or the abandonment of the pursuit all together. at times of strife there isn't much time to be thinking, which could be born from ""bad ideas"". this means that in essence, idealism is true, but that the applications of idealism can create a flow on affecting the material world, which then can hinder or benefit the development of ideas which then effects the material world again. they are interwoven and connected forces. the difference is the idealist beleives ideas directly affect the material, so that by having a good understanding of yourself and the world the material conditions will inherently advance.
