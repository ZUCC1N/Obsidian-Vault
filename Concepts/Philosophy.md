the study of questions relating to existence, knowledge, value, the mind or language that for the most part cannot be answered by science at the time of their formulation. 

It is made up of a collection of umbrella fields:
- [[Epistemology]]
- [[Metaphysics]]
- [[Logic]]
- [[Ethics]]
- [[Aesthetics]]
- [[Philosophy of Mind]]
- [[Philosophy of Language]]
- [[Political Philosophy]]
- [[Concepts/The History of Philosophy|The History of Philosophy]]
- [[Philosophical Meta-analysis]]

through the study of philosophy it is believed that history and the culture of humans can be better understood. they don't answer questions related to history or science per say, but give better context of the people of the time and our relationship to philosophy as a whole.
