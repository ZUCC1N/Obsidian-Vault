# Summary
due to a good and wise man's virtue being dependent only on himself, he cannot be robbed of it. and due to only caring for virtue and virtue alone, he thus cannot be injured or insulted. injury requires to be accepted, you have to claim you were injured to be truly, and thus since a stoic would never see harm intended as injury by another as an injury, it is impossible to be injured. he sees himself above human trifle and gossip, he doesn't even care for insults to listen enough, and does not despair at them so thus cannot be insulted. and also, is it really an insult to point out what is self evident? if you are so feeble to fold at observations, then you are a just a little bitch.
