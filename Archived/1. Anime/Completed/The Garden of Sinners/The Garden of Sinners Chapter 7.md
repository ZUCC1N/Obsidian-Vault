# Movie
Summary - in what appears to be a continuation of Shiki's actions in [[The Garden of Sinners Chapter 2|Movie 2]], murders where the victim is brutally slaughtered have began again in Ueno. the victim they find has been fucked up so much his body is strewn across an entire empty lot.

due to Shiki's lack of an alibi of being the murderer, Mikiya begins to investigate the murders in order to exonerate her. he tries to go to her apartment but she isn't there. he goes back to where he first saw her kill, remembering the events on the night she tried to kill him. you see the full scene of souren appearing, stopping shiki from killing Mikiya. Shiki then tries to kill herself by getting hit by a car in dishonour for trying to kill Mikiya.

daisuke stops by mikiya's house and tells him that the killer has been seen in a kimono at every scene and more than half the victims have been killed with LSD and weed on them.

mikiya goes to the dirty part of town to look for shiki, since if he can find the source of the drugs it may give him a lead. there he meets a random woman who wont sell him drugs, which leads him to see shiki down an alley. he chases her and then sees her fuck up 4 guys.

mikiya meets the woman again who tells him she also saw shiki. she informs him that the cocktail of weed and LSD is a combo which isnt fit for human bodies, but even then there is the stronger bloodchip denoted by its blood red stain markings (matches what daisuke showed mikiya) before he leaves he asks for the boss' name, which he is told but we dont hear.

touko visits all the crime scenes and laments that as the city gets brighter, people's shadows grow larger (both figuratively and literally with the context of jung). after that we are shown that scene from [[The Garden of Sinners Remix]] of shiki reflecting on why she is alone again.

shiki finds the actual murderer, being lio who got evil and fucked up by souren (was guy who shiki rejected in Movie 2 and saw at the end of [[The Garden of Sinners Chapter 6#**post credits**|Movie 6]]), having become obsessed with her and basically mirroring SHIKI.

mikiya goes to lio's house and finds out his obsession with shiki isn't a new thing. talks to him about his current state. he tries to offer lio help but he refuses saying he is beyond saving.

mikiya goes to shiki's house where she calls her own landline. she tells him she will kill lio because she still yearns for murder even without SHIKI. mikiya begs her to not before she hangs up and goes out to find lio. mikiya asks touko for help, saying she cant do much for the soul which is lios problem.

shiki leaks the weed stash and nearly kills lio, but cant, remembering the day she jumped in front of the car as she couldnt kill mikiya so decided to kill herself.

mikiya breaks in and is attacked by lio. lio drugs him but he refuses to dake bloodchip to survive. lio stabs him in the eye then goes and gets fucked up by shiki. mikiya finds shiki and tells her off for killing lio.

mikiya gets out of hospital and shiki accepts her past actions, finsihing her story (cause my theory is the story is about accepting ones animus/shadow.)
