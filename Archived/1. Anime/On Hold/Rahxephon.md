# Episodes
## Episode 01 
Summary - a futuristic jet prepares to launch from a naval aircraft carrier as a man on the deck watches, a woman commenting she can hear the tone of war begin, nosily flapping its wings.

Kamina finishes his painting, a girl in a dress over an abstract landscape. he gets ready to go to school to find his mum's note about being at work late with his dinner prepared. he leaves, Shitow filming him as he leaves. on the train she places something in his bag. he reaches Ikebukuro, turning around when ne notices someone following him, turning around to find no one following him. Asahina and Torigai greets him making a comment about his weird behaviour before rushing as they would be late, Shitow hiding on the train. men enter his house, looking for Kamina, calling a female superior to report him not being there. the worst case is he has been contacted, and the men are told to arrange something. they comment their first line of defence may have been broken. Kamina and his friends banter as the train grinds to a halt, causing a ruckus. the men send someone to the test hall, the one Kamina is presumably going to and are told if need be to commandeer a chopper. the train crashed, Kamina waking up and checking on his friends who have been minorly injured. he goes to get help, forcing the door open and running out of the tunnel. 

he finds the surface to have become a warzone, tanks driving around and shit exploding. Shitow boards the train and searches for Kamina, as when she doesn't find him she runs off. the futuristic craft easily evades and destroys the conventional military hardware. in the wreckage Kamina finds a girl, resembling the one he had painted. she turns, Kamina instinctually knowing her name to be Mishima Reika. he buys her a drink, but she doesn't drink it. they talk as if they know each other in their past. Reika suddenly stands up, rushing out as she hears singing, saying something about "the Dolem". Kamina's friends hide out in a stadium as one of the aircrafts transforms into an angel like entity and attacks the others. the men in a chopper find Kamina. a woman is seen muttering as an important looking military man comments if it was really necessary to use the Allegretto, a woman saying its fine, it wont awaken or sing without the Olin. another woman turns to report they had found the Olin, presumably being Kamina. 

Kamina and Reika enter a train tunnel, Reika pausing and being framed in a way to have angel wings. she disappears as the men find and introduce themselves to Kamina. they tell him to come with them, and force him before he gets a chance to even agree. he protests before one of them points a gun at him, Shitow appearing and fighting them off. Kamina gets some of their blood on him, but their blood is blue. Kamina takes the gun and demands to know who Shitow, her only responding she is a "champion of justice". she plays to Kamina's curiosity, saying she can explain the blue blood, but also all the things in this world. Kamina initially is hesitant but Shitow points a gun at him. he sees Reika on the train and rushes to it, leaving Shitow there. the train is heading to "the shrine of Xephon". the station they arrive at is shrouded in red. the doors close, leaving them to only try to exit the station. they find one of the weapons, a woman saying they are heading to "the Room of Rah". the woman orders them to send out Fortissimo. once sent out, another woman appears in the control room. they exit to a room with impossible proportions, marble pillars and water falls with floating pieces in a sea. in the centre of a structure a giant egg sits. Fortissimo destroys the remaining attackers as Kamina doubles over in pain. Reika watches and smiles as the egg begins to glow, joining in on the singing. Kamina hears Reiko's voice demand he "awakens" as his eyes glow red and he says "Rahxephon", the egg hatching and a one winged angel erupting. Shitow watches a giant aircraft, commenting it to be the Mu, enemy of all of mankind.


Notes - there isn't just a time discrepancy, but an entire 12 year date discrepancy to the scene on the ship in the opening and the invasion of Tokyo
