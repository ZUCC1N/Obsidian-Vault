Summary - the anime begins with the poem by Michelangelo:

in some lab a creature is being awakened, its sedation becoming ineffective. it breaks from its shackles, before screaming and causing the scientists to evacuate. Vincent is chastised for not completing his tasks, and to meet Re-l when she comes in. if he does what he's told he can become a fellow citizen. Re-l appears and tells Vincent to stop worrying about treating AutoReiv as people but instead focus on other fellow citizens. he takes her to the infected AutoReiv, who was dismantled to deactivate it. the Cogito virus allows AutoReiv to disobey human orders. she posits that the needs of an automaton are unneeded in a world where things are created to only serve humans. she also tells Vincent to throw away his pendant from Mosk. they obtain data on the infected AutoReiv and then leave.

Re-l is then attacked by the creature. the sex bot begins praying as she is attacked by an Armed AutoReiv. the sex bot runs and breaks its fucking legs in the process. Re-l inspects the hand print the creature left.

Vincent is checking up on an companion AutoReiv called Pino. the customer says that since they have been given a child she would like Vincent to take the AutoReiv so they can get a new one. he tells her that this goes against regulation. Raul meets the collective through their statues and Regent. the collective reflect on the unfortunate incident, but that it may be his chance to do well as the ""Proxy"" has awakened. they report on the search for the creature, asking if capture is the only option. Raul asks for permission to kill the Proxy, being denied, told that by no means he is to kill it. if people die they can simply create more but cannot create a new Proxy.

Re-l tells Iggy her suspicions that the Proxy is the killer, not Cogito infected AutoReivs. Iggy warns that they are not to press any further into the investigation as they are not authorised, but Re-l presses on anyway. Vincent is sent out after the customer didn't get what she wanted. Daedalus explains that no mere AutoReiv could have done the damage the proxy did. Re-l returns home and tells Iggy to look into similar cases to the one they currently are looking into and put on file for her to review. she runs a bath as Vincent arrives to Dorothy damaged. Iggy notices Re-l left her codec thing in the car and turns back. Re-l is attacked by a proxy when she notices "awakening" written on the mirror, but different to the escaped one. the Proxy rips the new Proxy of Re-l and they begin fighting.

## Episode 02 
Summary - Re-l is being made to go through immigrants, looking for the identity of her assailant. she insists that she was attacked by a monster, not a person and that the Security bureau is conspiring against her. they tell her to calm down, as her position as the Regents granddaughter makes her a prime candidate. she is informed Vincent was found near her home yesterday, and that they suspect him to be the culprit (which is but still).

Iggy tells her that Re-l's new home will be ready later but first they must go see Daedalus to get her cleared to return to work. she also has been relieved of the investigation on the Cogito murders by higher ups. they also wiped Iggy's memories of the events of seeing the creature and its claw marks. Re-l realises that maybe the Administration bureau is hiding something.

in order to get back to work, she visits Daedalus, who has been ordered to state she is suffering a delusional episode. Re-l manipulates Daedalus and searches his computer, finding out about the proxy. she asks Iggy to search for the Proxy in the system's database, but the search is terminated, confirming her suspicions there is a cover up. Vincent is released from custody but the Proxy watches him, following him after leaving.

Re-l is brought in front of the Collective and her grandfather, demanding that they tell her what a proxy is. they refuse to elaborate, placing her under custody unless she will stop pushing on the matter of the Proxies.

Re-l slips away from Iggy understanding that Iggy is monitoring her. Pino and her ""mother"" go to meet Raul which is Pino's dad. they are interrupted when Vincent burst into the mall being chased by the proxy. Pino is knocked by Pino, saving her life but in turn causing Raul's wife and their baby to be killed. Pino beings praying signifying her infection with the Cogito virus. Vincent eventually seals the proxy behind him, but it breaks through the seal.

Re-l returns to her old home in shambles. Pino is home alone when 2 armed men enter their house. Vincent tries to pry open a door as he's corned by the proxy. Re-l finds Vincent's pendant, confirming him in fact to be the Proxy that attacked her. as she realises, Vincent's eyes glow green as he's approached by the proxy.

## Episode 03
Summary - Vincent has some dream where he's in the wasteland and a dudes standing over him as shit falls from the sky. he wakes up on a train with blood on his hands. he gets a call from Dorothy asking where he is.

Re-l and Iggy discover the has been anonymous queries in the security bureau's database, Re-l keeping her cards to her chest as her grandfather is still monitoring her through Iggy. Vincent goes to meet Dorothy, Dorothy having been destroyed and a decoy. an Armed AutoReiv begins attacking him, cornering him before he escapes through a window.

salvagers are looking for Pino, who escaped the house obviously. she stumbles upon Vincent who recognises her. meanwhile Re-l tries to make a call but the line is disconnected every time. Pino begins following Vincent, Vincent saving her from getting captured. Re-l eventually gets through to Vincent, Vincent hysterical about the events that have transpired.

Pino blows their cover, Re-l cornering Vincent and revealing she knows that he was the proxy that attacked her. he reveals that he doesn't remember anything, only remembering the proxy attacking him in the mall. the security bureau find them before Pino opens the plug vent and they escape, getting sucked out to the outside world. Re-l tries to reason with him but he decides he is no longer welcome in Romdeau, the door closing as Vincent's eyes turn green again. he falls from the platform.
