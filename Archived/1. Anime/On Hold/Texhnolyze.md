# Episodes
## Episode 01 
Summary - Ichise walks down a dim corridor, having just finished a fight. he flashes back to it having brutalised his opponent getting lost in the moment of the crowd. he looks at something in a small vial, some organic mass suspended by a thread. he takes off his wraps having a shower and inspecting his wounds from the fight. a woman approaches him, handing him an envelope before pulling it back and saying something to him while touching him in a suggestive manner.

Kazuho descends into lux as Ichise. he starts cooking some food on the stairs down. meanwhile the woman starts to have sex with him while a man watches. the man leaves as the woman continues. at one point in the moment she stabs Ichise in the eye, making him punch her off him. Kazuho stares down the hole that the stairs circle around down to lux.

Ichise begins to wander lux, ending up in a sewer full of texhnolyzed limbs discarded. he takes out the money and the vial. men searching for him meanwhile Kazuho arrives in Lux, Ran watching him. meanwhile Keigo calls his wife, saying its quiet right now and he'll see her soon. next presumably Keigo's goons receive a call from the woman, telling them to hunt Ichise down for her. Kazuho meets ran. Ichise hides in a warehouse from the men searching for him, simply throwing the vial between his hands. Ran takes off her mask for Kazuho, getting a bit friendly with him before walking off, gesturing for him to follow her. 

Ichise pulls out a case with things in it. Ran leads Kazuho to Gabe territory, to meet her grandfather. her grandfather feeds the fishes as Kazuho arrives with Ran. suddenly Ran sees vision of the guard be shot as Ichise changes clothes and readies his gun. he hears a sound before his cover is riddled by bullets. her grandfather talks to Kazuho and why he is here as Ran has a vision of her grandfather being shot. he tells ran to hide as a man appears and spouts the salvation alliance's motto and shoots. Ichise runs from his attackers but is captured in the end. Kazuho shields the Elder and shoots the man. Ichise is dragged out before the men and disarmed. one pulls out a katana as the woman watches. the men grab his arm and stretch it out, the arm he had used to hit the woman and cut it off. he winces in pain as a lizard is shown, a symbol of regeneration.
Notes - the title of this episode "stranger" could refer to Kazuho who is a foreigner from lux but also Ichise being a stranger from the standards of being a lower caste and to the higher caste just an object.

## Episode 02 
Summary - the men continue to attack Gabe as Ichise has his arm cut off by the men. he freezes in shock before being kicked and his blood filling the payment. Keigo receives a call about the attack in Gabe, as the bodyguards there were his men. he goes tot he doc and gets his legs worked on, but she tells him his legs won't last forever. Ichise grabs his recently removed arms before seeing the woman. he begins screaming in rage and in a daze runs at her, going to punch her before his leg opposing his arm is cut off by the men. the car leaves the body guards as Keigo arrives to see what the ruckus is. the fight promoter tells him it was nothing to concern the Organo, the yakuza organisation in Lux, but he insists any violation where Organo men are used should be reported to them. he approaches Ichise and says nothing has happened in a while, but its a shame it had to end up like this. he calls Ichise scum, and he'll never receive the benefits of Raffia, he'll never become texhnolyzed. he cocks a gun and shoots Ichise, saying he still gets to decide who suffers or is put out of their misery.

Ran's grandfather asks Kazuho why he came to Lux, but says he just wanted to be there. he receives a call from the Organo to check up on them regarding the attack, but he says they have it handled. they should be more concerned about their immediate organisation. the elder tells Kazuo about ran's gift, being able to see the near future. Ichise having not been hit by the bullets ties his limbs to his stubs and drags himself along. Gabe is a village that was the first attempt to live outside the city, but most people by now have went back. the elder asks his name but Kazuho is cut off by a train going past. the elder questions if his arrival is a good or bad omen, Kazuho only saying ran would know. Ichise dragged himself into the warehouse and placed his limbs in a liquid, looking at the vial in his only working hand now.

the Organo discuss their movements against the salvation alliance. Keigo tells them to organise an attack, let the lower men blow off some steam. Ichise uses a walking stick to try to move through the city but falls down a set of stairs. Keigo calls the doc, informing her of a subject she may be interested in finding. at a market people notice Ichise, money in his mouth. someone takes pity on him and gives him food but he rejects it, dropping the money and walking away. he walks through the market as he overhears 2 men discussing the upcoming Alliance-Organo spat. they notice him and kick him over, walking away. Kazuo and Ran ride a train, making small talk as the Organo attack a salvation work site. Ichise stands at the base of a set of stairs, and hallucinates him self walking up them past him. he pauses, looking back at Ichise in a scornful manner. Ichise reaches out but is unable to follow, smashing his hand on the wall and watching the figure disappear. the doc finds him, smiling.

Notes - the title "Forfeiture" can refer to Ichise forfeiting his limbs for a momentary bit of payback but also Keigo forfeited the opportunity

## Episode 03
Summary - on the operating table, Ichise flashes back to the day his dad was hung on a construction site. his mother then falls ill, rapidly deteriorating in appearance and health and dies, a vial of her body tissue prepared for him by the hospital to remember her by. he remembers the events that led him to his current situation. Kazuho makes small talk with ran, saying he is indebted to her and her grandfather and will assist her in what ever way he can. he asks ran if she can see the future, and if she can see his to not tell anyone of his, especially himself. the doc eats a steak as Ichise fades in and out of consciousness, being in a body bag and on a feeding tube of some sort. he begins gasping, surprising the doc before she smiles. Keigo bathes as he receives a call, as his wife seems zoned out. 

ran and Kazuho arrive in Lux as the Doc does work. Ichise says his first word in the entire show, asking for water. the doc tells him he should be polite and say please and then gives him water when he listens, but pours it on him making him swallow what he can. Ichise tries to crawl out of the operating room before the Doc throws his severed arm at him and tells him he wont be going back to where she found him. Keigo receives a call again, the caller immediately hanging up when he answers. the doctor chastise him for thinking he could reattach the arm so tried preserving it, even raffia can't do that before Ichise attacks her. grabbing her by the throat. he tells her he wants nothing to do with raffia, before she tells him his house was burnt, he has no home left. he asks where the vial is, seeing it has been emptied. Ran and Kazuho disembark the train, going their separate ways, Ran disappearing in the steam from the train. 

Keigo leaves his house, a gunman from the Salvation Alliance. he goads the gunman, saying if they really want a war that badly and think they can replace the Organo. he asks the gunman if he knows why the leader, Kimata is texhnolyzed, or even if he was. he just wants raffia, not concerned with their flesh fundamentalism. the man starts to panic as Keigo approaches and begins to yell the alliance's motto and tires to shoot. but Keigo disarms him, uses him as a shield and uses the gun to shoot the assailant that was actually given a gun to kill him, the first shot being a blank. he scoffs that he'd only had his legs just serviced. Doc explains that she is tired of the conventional texhnolyzed limbs, and is trying go beyond it. she made him new limbs, but there is no way he could ever afford them in a life time. she used the tissue to create the biocircuit in the texhnolyze unit and thus living, not just a machine interfacing with nerves. with that he hits him in the head, knocking him off her. as Kazuho explores Lux, he passes by Keigo's car, heading to the Doc. Ichise insists he needs no machine, but the doc says if he doesn't use it no one will and it will go to waste. his mother will be just a waste. Keigo hits Ichise with the flat side of his sword as he goes for the Doc, reminding her its only cause he didn't kill him that he's here now, and he wouldn't dull his blade on just a dog so he is unharmed. he shows her his leg, insinuating he needs some more work done.
Notes - the title, Texnophile, most likely refers to the Doc who is obsessed in the development of pushing of the technology but also probably juxtaposes with the salvation Alliances "Texhnophobe" stance.
