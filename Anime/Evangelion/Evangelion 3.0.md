# Story
Shinji is saved from le cross box.  he is held, tied down with men pointing guns at him as he is transported. he is taken to Misato, with the crew of the ship glaring at him in disgust as they speak. Misato informs him of his situation, how he initiated near 3rd impact and will forever be held captive by WILLE. they are attacked. Asuka is deployed, Shinji being relieved Asuka is ok. he asks if he is being deployed by Misato just ignores him. he is told he isn't needed. confused, he asks how he can help, but is told by Misato he is to do nothing more from this point forward. he awakened for 12 seconds and is now unable to sync with an evangelion, having a sync rate of 0%. its been 14 years, nearly everyone despising him for causing near third impact. he asks why he and Asuka have not aged, being told its the curse of the Evas. rei appears and tries to take Shinji, but Misato threatens to kill him. she reveals they aren't with NERV, but WILLE, who's primary goal is to destroy the evangelions. Shinji goes with rei, and Misato hesitates to activate the choker, letting Shinji escape. Asuka remarks Shinji isn't an idiot, but a brat.

he is taken to NERV, being informed he is to share Evangelion 13 with Kaworu. he goes looking to thank rei for saving him, but she is completely docile, being unable to remember her past and emotionless. she has no passion or ambition or desire, only awaits for orders. Kaworu helps Shinji with the piano. Shinji asks how he could get better, but Kaworu says he should simply keep just doing, and eventually being good will come naturally. Shinji comments the star don't change, which gives him solace. wanting to know what happened while he was unconscious, Shinji asks Kaworu to show him the ruins of the city, being shown the red ruins full of failures of infinity. he remarks how most life forms adapt to their surroundings while lilin force the environment to adapt to them. shinji caused all of this in trying to save rei, and even if it was noble, he still did cause it. he denies it, saying he cant be blamed for being responsible if he cant do anything about it. kaowru agrees he cant change the past but he did ask to know. the lilin have placed the blame of this sin on to him, but no sin is unforgivable, there is always hope, no matter what.

shinji despite being told this enters a state of despair, regretting the actions he made that lead him to where he is now. Fuyutsuki tells him of the past, and how his father is sacrificng many things to try to right the world. shinji storms off in a rage, finding rei. when he asks why she didnt read the books, she says she wasnt ordered to. when he asks if he saved her, she respond she doesnt know. this sends shinji into a spiral, considering how no one wants him around or to pilot an eva, but still his father wishes him to pilot the eva. he questions what he has even beeing doing all his life, if he cant even do 1 thing right. Gendo readies the final piece of his plan as Kaworu goes to retrieve shinji from his room. he refuses to pilot on eva, but kaworu reminds him the past is the past, and holding onto it and letting it control him won't let him change what happened, but shinji responds that nothing good ever happens. he lost his trust anyone, feeling betrayed by the world. kaworu removes his choker, cheering him up and saying he cant do it without shinji. what shinji needs is hope, hope for the future and the forgiveness of others. 

they descend into central dogma to retrieve the 2 spears. Shinji asks why *she* is with them, she isn't even the real rei, which upsets rei in the first time she showed any level of serious emotion. Mari and Asuka interrupt them, trying to stop Shinji from piloting an eva, and kaowru realises they may be being fooled. he informs shinji of how the spears arent the spears they need, but shinji doesnt listen, removing the spears and starting 4th impact Kaworu was cast down to the 13th angel, initaitng the impact. shinji begins to blame himself but Kaworu explains he is at fault here. he apologises for not giving shinji the hapiness he had desired, but he'll fix it for shinji. he knows shinji will find peace one day, and a place of his own. kaowru smiles as the choker destroys his head

- everyone is a lot more jaded and serious than before. the entire vibe of the series has changed.
- its important to note that WILLE has seemed to changed materially as characters in ways (well besides mari but thats her point) while NERV are still holding onto 14 years before
	- gendo his plan
	- shinji rei and the past
	- rei hasn't progressed.
	- this probably represents a lack of agency to produce any change in their own lives at this stage.
